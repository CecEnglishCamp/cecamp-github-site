G24: Present Progressive

CEFR A1 = G24, A2 = G24+, B1 = G24++, B2 = G24+++

A1: 기본 현재진행형

We use "be + -ing" to talk about actions happening now.
Example: She is running.

A2: 부정문과 의문문

Negative: She is not running.
Question: Is she running?

B1: 시간표현과 함께 사용

Use with time expressions:
"She is running right now." / "They are studying at the moment."

B2: 임시 상황과 반복 행동

We can use present progressive for temporary or annoying repeated actions.
Example: "He is always losing his keys!"
