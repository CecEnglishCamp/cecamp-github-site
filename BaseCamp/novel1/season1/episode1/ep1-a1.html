📘 Season 1, Episode 1 – A1 Beginner

미소는 태백의 오래된 학교에 있다.
Miso is at an old school in Taebaek.
겨울이다. 강이 조용한 소리를 낸다.
It is winter. The river makes a soft sound.
그녀는 아빠의 오래된 상자를 연다.
She opens her dad’s old box.
안에는 광산 계획이 있다.
There is a plan about a mine.
미소는 광산으로 간다. 점프라는 로봇이 나온다.
Miso goes to the mine. A robot named Jump appears.
점프는 미소를 지하 400m로 데려간다.
Jump takes Miso 400 meters down underground.
로보라는 또 다른 로봇이 깨어난다.
Another robot, Robo, wakes up.
로보는 "이 광산은 마을을 따뜻하게 할 수 있어요"라고 말한다.
Robo says, “This mine can make the village warm.”
미소는 놀라고 말한다. “우리는 마을을 살릴 수 있어요!”
Miso is surprised and says, “We can save the village!”

📚 Target Vocabulary

  • school: a place to learn
  • box: something to hold things
  • plan: a paper with ideas
  • mine: a place under the ground
  • robot: a machine that moves
  • warm: not cold
  • scared: afraid
  • save: to help or rescue
  • cold: not warm
  • wake: to start or open

📝 Practice

  1. Miso is at an old ______. (school)
  2. The robot helps ______ the village. (save)
  3. It is ______ underground. (cold)
  4. Robo is a ______. (robot)

✍️ Writing Practice

  • Miso ________ the box. (opens)
  • Jump ________ Miso to the mine. (takes)
  • Robo ________ to make houses warm. (wants)
  • Miso ________ the village. (saves)

  1. Where is Miso? → At an old school ✅
  2. What does Robo say? → The mine can warm the village ✅
  3. What does Miso want to do? → Save the village ✅
