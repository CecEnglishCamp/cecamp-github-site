Season 1, Episode 1 – A1 Beginner

미소는 태백의 폐교에 갔다. 겨울이었다.
Miso is at an old school in Taebaek. It is winter.
황지천이 물소리를 냈다.
The river makes a soft sound.
미소는 아빠의 오래된 상자를 열었다. 상자에 설계도가 있었다.
She opens her dad’s old box. The box is rusty. She opens it. There is a plan inside.
‘T.O.B.I. 프로젝트’였다. 미소는 폐광으로 갔다.
The plan is about a mine. Miso goes to the mine.
점프가 나왔다. 점프는 빠른 로봇이다. 점프는 미소를 지하 400미터로 데려갔다.
A robot comes. It is Jump. Jump is fast with blue eyes. Jump takes Miso deep in the mine.
로보가 깨어났다. 로보는 로봇이다. 로보는 말했다. “이 폐광은 도움이 될 수 있어.”
Another robot, Robo, wakes up. Robo says, “This mine can help the village.”
미소는 기뻤다. “마을을 살릴 수 있어!”
Miso is happy. “We can save the village!”
갑자기 땅이 흔들렸다. 로보가 말했다. “위험한 신호야.”
The ground moves. Robo says, “Danger!”
미소는 무서웠지만 말했다. “우리가 마을을 깨울 거야!”
Miso is scared but says, “We will wake the village!”

📚 Target Vocabulary

  • school: a place to learn
  • rusty: old and red with rust
  • box: something to hold things
  • curious: wanting to know more
  • mine: a place under the ground
  • robot: a machine that moves
  • warm: not cold
  • happy: feeling good
  • scared: afraid
  • danger: not safe

Practice Using the Words

  1. Miso is at an old ________. (school)
  2. She opens a ________ box. (rusty)
  3. Miso is ________ to know more. (curious)
  4. Miso is ________ but wants to help. (scared)

📘 Target Grammar Points

  • Simple Present: "Miso is." → Miso is at the school.
  • Simple Past: "Miso opened." → Miso opened the box.
  • Can for Ability: "This can save." → The mine can make houses warm.

💬 Speaking Practice

Practice Present & Can:

  • Robo ________ (talk) about the mine. Miso ________ (help) the village.

Practice Past:

  • Jump ________ (come). Miso ________ (go) to the mine.

👂 Listening Practice

  • What does Miso find? a) Plan ✅ b) Toy
  • Where does Jump take Miso? a) Mine ✅ b) Park

✍️ Writing Practice

  • Miso ________ (open) the box.
  • Robo ________ (wake) up.
  • Jump ________ (take) Miso.
  • Miso ________ (say) we will wake the village.

🧠 Comprehension Questions

  • What does Miso find in the box? → A plan ✅
  • Who takes Miso to the mine? → Jump ✅
  • What does Robo say about the mine? → It can help the village ✅
  • What happens at the end? → The ground shakes ✅
