Season 1, Episode 1 – A1 Beginner

미소는 태백의 폐교에 갔다. 겨울이었다.
Miso is at an old school in Taebaek. It is winter.
황지천이 물소리를 냈다.
The river makes a soft sound.
미소는 아빠의 오래된 상자를 열었다. 상자에 설계도가 있었다.
She has her dad’s old box. The box is rusty. She opens it. There is a plan inside.
‘T.O.B.I. 프로젝트’였다. 미소는 폐광으로 갔다.
The plan is about a mine. Miso goes to the mine.
점프가 나왔다. 점프는 빠른 로봇이다. 점프는 미소를 지하 400미터로 데려갔다.
A robot comes. It is Jump. Jump is fast with blue eyes. Jump takes Miso deep in the mine. It is 400 meters down.
지하는 차가웠다. 로보가 깨어났다. 로보는 로봇이다.
The air is cold. Another robot, Robo, wakes up. Robo is a robot.
로보는 말했다. “이 폐광은 데이터센터가 될 수 있어. 마을을 따뜻하게 할 수 있어.”
Robo says, “This mine can help the village. It can make houses warm.”
미소는 놀랐다. “마을을 살릴 수 있어!”
Miso is happy. “We can save the village!”
갑자기 땅이 흔들렸다. 로보가 말했다. “위험한 신호야.”
The ground moves. Robo says, “Danger!”
미소는 무서웠지만 말했다. “우리가 마을을 깨울 거야!”
Miso is scared but says, “We will wake the village!”
황지천 물소리가 들렸다. 미소는 점프와 로보를 보며 힘을 냈다.
The river makes a sound outside. Miso sees Jump and Robo and feels strong.

📚 Target Vocabulary

  • school: a place to learn
  • box: something to hold things
  • rusty: old and red with rust
  • plan: a paper with ideas
  • mine: a place under the ground
  • robot: a machine that moves
  • warm: not cold
  • happy: feeling good
  • scared: afraid
  • danger: not safe

📘 Target Grammar Points

  • Simple Present: "Miso is." (facts or habits)
  • Simple Past: "Miso opened." (past actions)

💬 Speaking Practice

Practice Simple Present

  • Robo ________ (talk) about the mine. Miso ________ (want) to save the village.

Practice Simple Past

  • Jump ________ (come). Miso ________ (go) to the mine.

👂 Listening Practice

  • What does Miso find? a) Plan ✅ b) Toy
  • Where does Jump take Miso? a) Mine ✅ b) Park

✍️ Writing Practice

  • Miso ________ (open) the box.
  • Robo ________ (wake) up.
  • Jump ________ (take) Miso.
  • Miso ________ (say) we will wake the village.

Write 2 sentences about the story.
Example: Miso found a plan. Robo talked about the mine.

🧠 Comprehension Questions

  1. What does Miso find in the box? → A plan ✅
  2. Who takes Miso to the mine? → Jump ✅
  3. What does Robo say about the mine? → It can help the village ✅
  4. What happens at the end? → The ground moves, and Miso wants to wake the village ✅

🧾 Wrap-up Summary

What We Learned

  • Vocabulary: school, box, rusty, plan, mine, robot, warm, happy, scared, danger
  • Grammar: Simple Present (Miso is), Simple Past (Miso opened)
  • Practice: Talking about actions now and in the past

Let’s Try! Example: “Miso opened the box. Robo woke up.”
