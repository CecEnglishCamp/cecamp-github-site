Season 1, Episode 1 – A1 Beginner

미소는 태백의 폐교에 갔다. 겨울이었다.
Miso is at an old school in Taebaek. It is winter.
황지천이 물소리를 냈다.
The river makes a soft sound.
미소는 아빠의 오래된 상자를 열었다. 상자에 설계도가 있었다.
She opens her dad’s old box. The box is rusty. She opens it. There is a plan inside.
‘T.O.B.I. 프로젝트’였다. 미소는 폐광으로 갔다.
The plan is about a mine. Miso goes to the mine.
점프가 나왔다. 점프는 빠른 로봇이다. 점프는 미소를 지하 400미터로 데려갔다.
A robot comes. It is Jump. Jump is fast. Jump takes Miso 400 meters underground.
지하는 차가웠다. 로보가 깨어났다. 로보는 로봇이다.
The air is cold. Robo wakes up. Robo is a robot.
로보는 말했다. “이 폐광은 데이터센터가 될 수 있어. 마을을 따뜻하게 할 수 있어.”
Robo says, “This mine can be a data center. It can warm the village.”

📚 Target Vocabulary

  • school: a place to learn
  • rusty: old and red with rust
  • box: something to hold things
  • curious: wanting to know more
  • mine: a place under the ground
  • robot: a machine that moves
  • warm: not cold
  • happy: feeling good
  • scared: afraid
  • danger: not safe
Practice Using the Words:
1. Miso is at an old school.
2. She opens a rusty box.
3. Miso is curious to know more.
4. Miso is scared but wants to help.
Practice Simple Present and Can:
Miso is at the school. The mine can make houses warm.
Robo talks about the mine. Miso helps the village.
