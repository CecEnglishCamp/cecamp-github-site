Season 1, Episode 1 – B2 Upper-Intermediate

태백, 2025년 겨울. 폐교된 장성초 앞에 선 미소는 아버지의 녹슨 철제 상자를 들고 있었다.
In Taebaek’s winter of 2025, Miso lingered before the forsaken Jangseong Elementary School, clutching her father’s corroded metal box.
황지천은 얼지 않은 물소리를 내며 흐르고, 바람은 유리처럼 차갑게 뺨을 스쳤다.
The Hwangjeecheon River’s gentle murmur drifted nearby, while a biting breeze grazed her cheeks.
그녀는 무릎을 꿇고 상자를 열었다. 그 안엔 ‘장성탄광 열 회수 시스템’이란 제목의 바랜 종이들이 있었다.
Kneeling, she pried open the lid, unveiling faded manuscripts titled “T.O.B.I. Project: Jangseong Mine Heat Recovery System.”
익숙하고도 낯선 아버지의 붓글씨는, 종이 속에서 말을 건네는 듯했다.
Her father’s calligraphy, both unfamiliar and achingly familiar, stirred her—as if the papers might respond.
“출입금지”라는 팻말이 붙은 녹슨 철문 앞에서, 미소는 문을 밀고 들어갔다.
Intrigued, Miso approached the derelict mine, its gate marked with a crumbling “No Entry” sign. She shoved it open.
그 순간, 네 다리로 착지한 작은 로봇이 튀어나왔다. 파란 렌즈 눈, 홀로그램: [T.O.B.I. UNIT-2 / JUMP]
Abruptly, a nimble robot, Jump, leaped onto a metal surface, its azure eyes gleaming. It projected a hologram: [T.O.B.I. UNIT-2 / JUMP]
미소는 몇 걸음 망설이다가 결국 따라갔다. 심장은 쿵쾅거렸다.
Miso hesitated, then followed, her pulse racing.
리프트는 덜컹이며 내려갔다: 80m, 180m, 400m. 지하 400m 공기는 습하고 차가웠다.
The lift creaked downward: 80m, 180m, 400m. At 400 meters, damp air enveloped her.
벽에 장착된 장치를 작동시키자, 미세한 진동과 함께 벽이 열리고 로보가 움직이기 시작했다.
Jump sprang to a wall, activating a cube-like device. With a hum, a panel slid open, and Robo stirred.
“T.O.B.I. UNIT-1: ROBO. 대기일수: 3,672일”
“T.O.B.I. UNIT-1: ROBO. Standby: 3,672 days,” it intoned.
“지하수 9.8도, 하루 유출량 5,000톤. 냉각 효율 92.4%.”
“Groundwater: 9.8°C, 5,000 tons daily. Cooling efficiency 92.4%.”
“…이거, 데이터센터야?”
“A data center?”
“정확합니다. 에스토니아, 핀란드, 노르웨이 사례 기반.”
“Correct. Estonia, Finland, Norway precedents validate feasibility.”
“마을 난방 전환 효율 86%.”
“Village heating efficiency 86%.”
그때, 진동이 땅을 울리고, 로보의 눈이 깜빡였다.
A tremor jolted the ground. Robo’s eyes flickered rapidly.
“T.O.B.I. 프로토콜 위반. 외부 신호 감지.”
“Warning: unidentified signal detected. T.O.B.I. protocol violation.”
“무언가 있었어. 사람은 아니야.”
“Something’s there—not human,” it signaled.
“우리가 이 마을을 깨울 거야. 시작은 여기야.”
“We’ll awaken this village. It starts here.”

📚 Target Vocabulary

  • forsaken: abandoned, left alone
  • clutch: to hold tightly
  • murmur: a soft, quiet sound
  • unveil: to reveal something hidden
  • derelict: old and broken
  • nimble: quick and light in movement
  • gleam: to shine brightly
  • affirm: to state strongly
  • jolt: a sudden shake
  • unwavering: steady and strong

Practice Using the Words

  1. Miso stood at a ________ school. (forsaken)
  2. She ________ the box tightly. (clutched)
  3. Jump’s eyes ________ brightly. (gleamed)
  4. Miso’s resolve was ________. (unwavering)

📘 Target Grammar Points

  • Simple Past: Miso pried, Robo stirred to life.
  • Reported Speech: Robo affirmed that it was correct.
  • Adverbs of Manner: Miso murmured softly, Jump darted nimbly.
  • Modal Verbs for Possibility: This could save the village, They might uncover a threat.
  • Basic Conditional: If we awaken the village, it will thrive.
