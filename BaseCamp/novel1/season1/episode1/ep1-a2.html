📘 Season 1, Episode 1 – A2 Level

태백, 2025년 겨울. 미소는 오래된 학교 앞에 서 있다.
In Taebaek, winter 2025, Miso stands at an old school.
황지천이 조용하게 흐른다.
The Hwangjeecheon River makes a soft sound.
그녀는 아빠의 녹슨 상자를 들고 있다.
She holds her dad’s rusty box.
상자를 열고 오래된 종이를 찾는다.
She opens it and finds old papers.
그 종이들은 광산을 위한 ‘T.O.B.I. 프로젝트’ 계획서이다.
The papers are a plan called “T.O.B.I. Project” for a mine.
미소는 궁금해서 광산으로 간다.
Miso is curious and walks to the mine.
문에는 ‘출입금지’ 표지판이 있다. 그녀는 문을 민다.
The gate is old with a “No Entry” sign. She pushes it open.
차가운 공기가 나온다. 로봇 점프가 나타난다.
Cold air comes out. A robot, Jump, appears.
점프는 빠르고 푸른 눈을 가지고 있다. 미소를 광산 깊이 데려간다.
Jump is fast with bright blue eyes. It takes Miso deep into the mine.
로봇 로보가 깨어나고 말한다. “이 광산은 데이터 센터가 될 수 있어요.”
Another robot, Robo, wakes up and says, “This mine can be a data center.”
갑자기 땅이 흔들린다. 미소는 무섭지만 말한다. “우리는 마을을 깨울 거예요!”
Suddenly, the ground shakes. Miso is scared but says, “We will wake the village!”

📚 Target Vocabulary

  • winter: cold season
  • soft: quiet or gentle
  • rusty: old and red with rust
  • curious: wanting to know more
  • push: to move something forward
  • cold: not warm
  • wake: to start or open
  • heat: to make warm
  • shake: to move quickly
  • excited: very happy

📝 Practice Using the Words

  1. It is ________ in Taebaek. (winter)
  2. The river makes a ________ sound. (soft)
  3. Miso is ________ to know more. (curious)
  4. Miso is ________ to save the village. (excited)

📘 Grammar Points

  • Simple Past: Miso opened the box.
  • Reported Speech: Robo said that it could be a data center.
  • Can for Ability: The mine can heat houses.

💬 Speaking Practice

  • Jump ________ (take) Miso. Miso ________ (say) they would wake the village.
  • Robo ________ (make) a data center. Miso ________ (help) the village.

👂 Listening Practice

  • What does Miso find? a) Plan ✅ b) Book
  • What shakes? a) Ground ✅ b) Sky

✍️ Writing Practice

  • Miso ________ (open) the box.
  • Robo ________ (say) it could be a data center.
  • Jump ________ (take) Miso.
  • Miso ________ (say) they would wake the village.

🧠 Comprehension Questions

  • Where is Miso when she opens the box? → At an old school ✅
  • Who takes Miso to the mine? → Jump, a robot ✅
  • What does Robo say about the mine? → It can be a data center ✅
  • What happens at the end? → The ground shakes, and Miso wants to wake the village ✅

🧾 Wrap-up Summary

What We Learned: Vocabulary (winter, soft, rusty...), grammar (simple past, reported speech), and comprehension activities.

Try saying: “Miso opened the box. Robo said that it could be a data center.”
