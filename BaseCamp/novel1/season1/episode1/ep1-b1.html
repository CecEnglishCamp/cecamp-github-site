Season 1, Episode 1 – B1 Intermediate

태백, 2025년 겨울. 황지천은 얼어붙지 않은 물소리를 내며 흐르고 있었다.
In Taebaek, winter 2025, the Hwangjeecheon River flowed with a soft sound.
하늘에서 부는 바람은 얇은 유리조각처럼 미소의 뺨을 스쳤다.
A cold wind touched her face.
그녀는 폐교 앞에 서 있었다. 손에는 녹슨 철제 상자가 들려 있었다.
She stood in front of the empty school. She held her dad’s rusty metal box tightly.
상자를 열자 오래된 종이 뭉치가 나왔다. ‘T.O.B.I. 프로젝트 – 장성탄광 열 회수 시스템’
She opened it and found old papers. They were plans for the T.O.B.I. Project about a mine’s heat system.
미소는 궁금해졌다. 녹슨 철문을 밀고 탄광 안으로 들어갔다.
Miso became curious and pushed the rusty gate open. She walked into the old mine.
갑자기 로봇 하나가 나타났다. 이름은 점프였다. 파란 눈이 반짝였다.
Suddenly, a robot appeared. Its name was Jump. It had bright blue eyes.
점프는 미소를 데리고 지하로 내려갔다. 리프트는 400m까지 내려갔다.
Jump took Miso deep into the mine. The lift went down to 400 meters.
거긴 차고 습했다. 오래된 기계들과 파이프가 있었다. 석탄과 기름 냄새가 났다.
It was cold and wet. Old machines and pipes were everywhere. She smelled coal and oil.
다른 로봇 하나가 깨어났다. “나는 로보. T.O.B.I. UNIT-1.”
Another robot, Robo, woke up. “I am Robo. T.O.B.I. UNIT-1.”
로보는 말했다. “이 광산은 데이터센터가 될 수 있어요. 마을을 데울 수 있어요.”
Robo said, “This mine can be a data center. It can heat the village.”
미소는 말했다. “이거면 마을을 살릴 수 있어!”
Miso said, “This can save our village!”
그때 땅이 흔들렸다. 로보가 경고했다. “위험 신호 감지!”
Then the ground shook. Robo warned, “There is a danger signal!”
점프가 어둠 속을 살폈다. “뭔가 있어. 사람이 아니야.”
Jump looked into the dark. “Something is there, not a person.”
미소의 심장이 빨리 뛰었다. “이건 단순한 폐광이 아니야.”
Miso’s heart beat fast. “This is not just a mine.”
그녀는 로보와 점프를 보며 말했다. “우리가 이 마을을 깨울 거야!”
She looked at Robo and Jump. “We will wake this village!”

📚 Vocabulary

  • empty: with nothing inside
  • soft: quiet or gentle
  • rusty: old and red with rust
  • curious: wanting to know more
  • push: to move something forward
  • cold: not warm
  • warn: to tell about danger
  • excited: very happy
  • shake: to move quickly
  • strong: powerful

📝 Practice

  1. Miso stands at an ________ school. (empty)
  2. The river makes a ________ sound. (soft)
  3. Miso is ________ to know more. (curious)
  4. The ground starts to ________. (shake)

📘 Grammar

  • Simple Past: Miso pushed the box.
  • Reported Speech: Robo warned that there was a danger signal.
  • Adverbs of Manner: Jump moved quickly.
  • Can for Ability: The mine can heat houses.

💬 Speaking Practice

  • Jump took Miso. Miso said they would wake the village.
  • Robo warned seriously. This can save the village.

✍️ Writing Practice

  • Miso ________ (push) the box.
  • Robo ________ (warn) that there was a danger signal.
  • Jump ________ (take) Miso.
  • Miso ________ (say) they would wake the village.

🧠 Comprehension Check

  1. Where is Miso when she finds the papers? → At an empty school ✅
  2. Who takes Miso into the mine? → Jump, a robot ✅
  3. What does Robo say about the mine? → It can be a data center ✅
  4. What happens at the end? → The ground shakes, and Miso wants to wake the village ✅
