📘 Season 1, Episode 1 – B1 Intermediate

태백, 2025년 겨울. 폐교된 장성초 앞에 선 미소는 아버지의 녹슨 철제 상자를 들고 있었다.
In Taebaek’s winter of 2025, Miso lingered before the forsaken Jangseong Elementary School, clutching her father’s corroded metal box.
황지천은 얼지 않은 물소리를 내며 흐르고, 바람은 유리처럼 차갑게 뺨을 스쳤다.
The Hwangjeecheon River’s gentle murmur drifted nearby, while a biting breeze grazed her cheeks.
그녀는 무릎을 꿇고 상자를 열었다. 그 안엔 ‘장성탄광 열 회수 시스템’이란 제목의 바랜 종이들이 있었다.
Kneeling, she pried open the lid, unveiling faded manuscripts titled “T.O.B.I. Project: Jangseong Mine Heat Recovery System.”
익숙하고도 낯선 아버지의 붓글씨는, 종이 속에서 말을 건네는 듯했다.
Her father’s calligraphy, both unfamiliar and achingly familiar, stirred her—as if the papers might respond.

📚 Target Vocabulary

  • empty: with nothing inside
  • soft: quiet or gentle
  • rusty: old and red with rust
  • curious: wanting to know more
  • push: to move something forward
  • cold: not warm
  • warn: to tell about danger
  • excited: very happy
  • scared: afraid
  • strong: powerful

📝 Practice Using the Words

  1. Miso stood at an ________ school. (empty)
  2. The river made a ________ sound. (soft)
  3. Miso was ________ to know more. (curious)
  4. Miso felt ________ but strong. (scared)

📘 Grammar Points

  • Simple Past: Miso pushed the box.
  • Reported Speech: Robo warned that there was a danger signal.
  • Adverbs of Manner: Miso said bravely. Jump moved quickly.
  • Can for Ability: The mine can heat houses.

💬 Speaking Practice

  • Jump ________ (take) Miso. Miso ________ (say) they would wake the village.
  • Robo ________ (warn) seriously. This ________ (save) the village.

👂 Listening Practice

  • What did Miso find? a) Plan ✅ b) Letter
  • What shook? a) Ground ✅ b) Sky

✍️ Writing Practice

  • Miso ________ (push) the box.
  • Robo ________ (warn) that there was a danger signal.
  • Jump ________ (take) Miso.
  • Miso ________ (say) they would wake the village.

🧠 Comprehension Questions

  • Where was Miso when she found the papers? → At an empty school ✅
  • Who led Miso into the mine? → Jump, a robot ✅
  • What did Robo say about the mine? → It can be a data center ✅
  • What happened at the end? → The ground shook; she said they would wake the village ✅

🧾 Wrap-up Summary

What We Learned: Vocabulary (empty, soft, rusty...), grammar (simple past, reported speech), and comprehension activities.
