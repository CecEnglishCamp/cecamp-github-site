Season 1, Episode 1 – B1 Intermediate

Light Lives Under the Village

In Taebaek, winter 2025, Miso stood in front of an empty school. The river flowed nearby with a soft sound. A cold wind touched her face. Miso opened her father’s old rusty box. Inside, she found a plan for a “T.O.B.I. Project”—a mine’s heat system. Miso was curious and entered the old mine. She pushed the rusty gate open. Suddenly, a robot appeared. “My name is Jump!” It was fast with bright blue eyes. Jump took Miso deep into the mine. Another robot, Robo, started to move. Robo said, “This mine can be a data center. It can heat the village.” Miso was excited. “This can save our village!” The ground shook. Robo warned, “There is a danger signal!” Jump looked into the dark. Miso’s heart beat fast. “This is not just a mine,” she said. The river’s sound outside was like a heartbeat.

📚 Target Vocabulary
  • empty: with nothing inside
  • rusty: old and red with rust
  • curious: wanting to know more
  • data center: a place that stores data
  • danger signal: warning sign
Quiz 1: What did Miso find inside the old box?
A plan for a “T.O.B.I. Project” (mine’s heat system).
Quiz 2: How did Miso feel when the ground shook?
She was excited but also scared.
