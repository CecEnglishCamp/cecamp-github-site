Season 1, Episode 1 – A2 Elementary

Light Lives Under the Village

In Taebaek, winter 2025, Miso stands at an old school. The Hwangjeecheon River makes a soft sound. She holds her dad’s rusty box. She opens it and finds old papers. The papers are a plan called “T.O.B.I. Project” for a mine. Miso is curious and walks to the mine. The gate is old with a “No Entry” sign. She pushes it open. Cold air comes out. A robot, Jump, appears. Jump is fast with bright blue eyes. It takes Miso deep into the mine, 400 meters down. The air is cold and wet. Old machines and pipes are around. Another robot, Robo, wakes up. Robo says, “This mine can be a data center. It can cool machines and heat the village. Other places did this.” Miso is excited. “This can save our village!” Suddenly, the ground shakes. Robo says, “There is a danger sign!” Jump looks into the dark. Miso is scared but says, “We will wake the village!” The river’s sound is loud outside.

📚 Target Vocabulary

  • winter: cold season
  • soft: quiet or gentle
  • rusty: old and red with rust
  • curious: wanting to know more
  • push: to move something forward
  • cold: not warm
  • wake: to start or open
  • heat: to make warm
  • shake: to move quickly
  • excited: very happy

Practice

  1. It is winter in Taebaek.
  2. The river makes a soft sound.
  3. Miso is curious to know more.
  4. Miso is excited to save the village.

📘 Grammar

  • Simple Past: "Miso opened." (past actions)
  • Reported Speech: Robo said that it could be a data center.
  • Can for Ability: "This can save."

💬 Speaking & Listening

  • Practice: "Miso opened the box. Robo said that it could be a data center."
  • ▶ Listen to the story

🧠 Comprehension

  1. Where is Miso when she opens the box? At an old school
  2. Who takes Miso to the mine? Jump, a robot
  3. What does Robo say about the mine? It can be a data center
  4. What happens at the end? The ground shakes, and Miso wants to wake the village
