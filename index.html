About CEC English Camp

We provide grammar, reading, speaking, and listening in one integrated experience…

At CEC English Camp, we believe learning should be meaningful and joyful. That’s why our programs combine storytelling, real-world topics, and interactive AI tools. Our curriculum is based on CEFR levels (A1–B2) and uses short novels, grammar coaching, and speaking tasks to build confidence in young learners.

Whether your child is just beginning or already speaking, our step-by-step structure adapts to their level. Each week includes themed vocabulary, real-life communication practice, and guidance from our warm team of story-based learning coaches.
