About Our Program

CEC English Camp helps children explore English through imagination and interactive storytelling. From A1 to B2, we provide grammar, vocabulary, speaking and confidence-building activities for every level.

Our Story

One day, a girl met a robot with glowing eyes. That moment began a magical journey of learning, helping, and growing together. At English Camp, we bring imagination and language to life!
