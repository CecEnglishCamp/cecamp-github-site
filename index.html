About CEC English Camp

Our journey begins with Episode 1: The Hidden Warehouse Files — a short novel that blends action, humor, and heartwarming moments with real English learning. Students follow the adventures of a lovable learning companion robot designed to help children overcome everyday challenges. Every episode leaves them thinking: "I wish I had a robot like that!"

This series inspires kids to revisit the story whenever life feels hard, find courage, and recharge emotionally. Each story episode becomes a springboard for practicing all four core skills: Reading, Listening, Speaking, and Writing.

We've developed a full curriculum around each short novel episode, helping learners grow naturally and joyfully. And in the near future, they'll even be able to interact with AI-powered versions of their story robots.

Why It Works

Real emotional connection. High-impact storytelling. Smart, structured practice. CEC isn’t just another English camp — it’s a place where learners build resilience, confidence, and a lifelong love of language.
