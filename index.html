Engaging English Learning

Discover the joy of language through stories

At CEC English Camp, we ignite a passion for English in kids through captivating short novels. Our innovative approach focuses on grammar while making learning fun and engaging. With a supportive environment, children develop their language skills, boost their confidence, and build a lifelong love for reading. Join us in transforming the way young learners experience English!

📘 Short Novel, Title: Robo, Jump & Me – Season 1 / Episode 1

어느 날 공이 사라졌습니다. 미소는 공을 찾기 위해 조용한 창고로 향합니다. 그곳에서 예상치 못한 빛나는 상자를 발견하게 되는데...

이 이야기에는 상상력, 우정, 모험이 함께 펼쳐집니다. 아이들의 창의성과 상상력을 자극하기 위한 단편 시리즈로 구성되어 있으며, 각 에피소드는 짧지만 깊은 여운을 남깁니다.

💬 What They Say

Jennie – Fremont School District

As an educator, I appreciate the innovative approach that CecEnglishCamp takes in teaching English. The use of short novels to explain grammar is both creative and effective. My daughter has improved her writing and comprehension skills significantly. The camp's supportive environment encourages her to express herself confidently. I am grateful for the positive impact it has had on her learning journey.

Ryan – Huang Family

CecEnglishCamp has been a fantastic experience for my son. He not only learned English grammar but also developed a love for reading through the captivating stories. The instructors are patient and skilled, making every lesson engaging and fun. My son has gained confidence in his language abilities and looks forward to each class.

David – David Family

CecEnglishCamp has transformed my child's understanding of English in such a short time. The engaging short novels not only captivate their attention but also reinforce grammar concepts effectively. My child looks forward to each session, excited to explore new stories and learn.
