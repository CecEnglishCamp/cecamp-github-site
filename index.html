Our Programs

Program 1: Mastery Through Narrative

Experience English grammar and vocabulary in action through immersive storytelling.

Dear Parents,

At CEC, we believe that language is best learned when it is lived. Our foundational program, Mastery Through Narrative, brings grammar, vocabulary, and sentence structure alive through storytelling.

Your child will create and reimagine stories, learning sentence structure and parts of speech in a fun and relatable way. Children move beyond memorization and begin to communicate their own thoughts with growing independence and delight.

The CEC English Camp Team

Program 2: Integrated Literacy

Deepen reading and writing skills while developing empathy and critical thinking.

Dear Parents,

We proudly introduce Integrated Literacy & Critical Thinking—a program designed for a deeper connection with language through reading comprehension, writing, and discussion.

This module ensures that your child not only understands English but can also use it with clarity and insight.

The CEC English Camp Team

Program 3: Fluency & Confident Expression

Build real-world speaking confidence through interactive activities and challenges.

Dear Parents,

Our milestone program is Fluency & Confident Expression, where young learners step into English-speaking roles with poise.

By the end, students not only speak—they connect and shine.

Yours sincerely,
The CEC English Camp Team
