Episode 1: The Hidden Warehouse Files

Our journey begins with Episode 1: The Hidden Warehouse Files — a short novel that blends action, humor, and heartwarming moments...

Students follow the adventures of a lovable learning companion robot designed to help children overcome everyday challenges. Every episode leaves them thinking: "I wish I had a robot like that!" The story supports learning all four core skills: Reading, Listening, Speaking, and Writing.
