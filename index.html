More About Our Camp

At CEC English Camp, our mission is to nurture children's imagination and confidence through English. With original storylines like "Robot & Me" and "Jump & Me", learners experience language in action. Each level includes grammar, listening, speaking, and writing practice. Our instructors and AI partners support learners step-by-step, in class and online.
