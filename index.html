About CEC English Camp

CEC English Camp is a unique, story-driven language program designed for elementary and middle school learners. We believe that language acquisition happens most naturally when students are engaged emotionally and intellectually. That's why our programs combine beautifully illustrated stories with core grammar, speaking, and writing practice.
