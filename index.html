About CEC English Camp

We provide grammar, reading, speaking, and listening in one integrated experience...

At CEC English Camp, we believe learning should be meaningful and joyful. That’s why our programs combine storytelling, real-world topics, and interactive AI tools. Our curriculum is based on CEFR levels (A1–B2) and uses short novels, grammar coaching, and speaking tasks to build confidence in young learners.

Whether your child is just beginning or already speaking, our step-by-step structure adapts to their level. Each week includes themed vocabulary, real-life communication practice, and guidance from our warm team of story-based learning coaches.

Season 1, Episode 1: The Hidden Warehouse Files

Our journey begins with Episode 1…

Miso finds an old robot in her grandfather’s dusty warehouse. But this robot is no ordinary machine—it holds the key to a forgotten mission. From this moment on, Miso’s life is never the same.

Each episode of our novel series builds language naturally, while keeping learners excited for what happens next. From vocabulary highlights to grammar-in-context, students won’t even realize they’re learning.

Programs

Interactive English Learning

Engaging activities that teach English through fun. My child looks forward to each session.

Children participate in story roleplays, puzzles, voice-based games, and AI chatbot speaking practice. We focus on confidence and connection first, building fluency step by step.

Reading Comprehension Programs

Short novels that captivate attention and reinforce grammar naturally.

Our story modules follow engaging characters across episodes. Each one targets specific grammar points and vocabulary through meaningful use—not drills or translation. Reading becomes a joy.

English Grammar

Clear and fun structure to improve writing and speaking skills.

From be-verbs to complex sentence structures, our curriculum introduces grammar using charts, visuals, examples, and creative speaking activities. Students learn to use it—not just memorize rules.
